When asked to provide more context about who was especially vulnerable to restrictions on their rights to opinion and expression by the government or its agents in 2019, our respondents mentioned all of the following:

-	Journalists, especially independent media outlets including Efecto Cocuyo, El Pitazo, and Punto de Corte; owners of media outlets
-	Politicians and community leaders
-	Medical professionals, particularly doctors, nurses, and medical staff in hospitals
-	People with health conditions, especially the elderly, women, children, and people with disabilities
-	People who are in opposition to or critical of the ruling government, especially politicians, journalists, academics, members of trade unions, opposition parties, and protesters, dissidents
-	Civil society organizations, especially those that focus on human rights
-	Representatives of professional organisations, especially medical and educational associations
-	People who have been negatively impacted by humanitarian situations 
-	Individuals associated with the National Assembly
